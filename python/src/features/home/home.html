Kintsugi

The art of precious scars

Kintsugi (金継ぎ) is the Japanese art of repairing broken pottery by mending the cracks with lacquer dusted or mixed with powdered gold. The philosophy treats breakage and repair as part of the history of an object, rather than something to disguise.
